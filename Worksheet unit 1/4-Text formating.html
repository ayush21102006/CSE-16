Main Heading (h1)

This is the top-level heading, usually used for the page title.


Subheading (h2)

This heading is slightly smaller and often used for major sections.


Section Heading (h3)

This heading is used for subsections within an h2 section.


Subsection Heading (h4)

This heading provides more detailed structure under h3.


Minor Heading (h5)

This heading is rarely used, but helps in deep structuring.


Smallest Heading (h6)

This is the smallest heading, often used for notes or references.
